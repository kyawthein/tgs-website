Protecting Executive Personals

Our information team identified an ongoing threat targeting our client, a critical supplier to the Myanmar Air Force. Immediate action was taken to mitigate potential risks and ensure the safety of both personnel and operations.

The Threat Assessment

Our intelligence team detected credible threats targeting a critical defense industry supplier with significant contracts with the Myanmar Air Force. The nature of the client's business made them a high-value target for various threat actors seeking to disrupt defense supply chains or gain unauthorized access to sensitive information.

The threat level was assessed as imminent and credible, requiring immediate protective measures to safeguard executive personnel and ensure operational continuity during this critical period.

⚠️ Critical Risk Factors

High-profile defense contractor status, sensitive government contracts, executive exposure in public settings, and potential for both physical and information security threats.

TGS Protective Response

Upon threat identification, TGS immediately activated our comprehensive executive protection protocol:

Actions Implemented

  • 24/7 Online Media Monitoring: Established dedicated monitoring team to track and assess threat-related activity across all digital platforms
  • Round-the-Clock Executive Protection: Deployed professional protective security details for all client executives operating in the affected area
  • Multi-Agency Coordination: Coordinated with local security personnel and government officials to align on risk assessments and response measures
  • Threat Intelligence Analysis: Continuous assessment of threat evolution and potential escalation scenarios
  • Secure Communication Protocols: Implemented enhanced communication security for all executive movements and business operations
  • Emergency Response Planning: Prepared rapid evacuation and emergency response procedures

Operational Excellence

During the seven-day critical period, TGS maintained the highest levels of protective security while allowing the client to continue essential business operations. Our team provided:

  • Continuous Monitoring: 24/7 surveillance and threat assessment capabilities
  • Executive Mobility Security: Secure transportation and location protection for all movements
  • Real-time Intelligence: Immediate updates on threat status and environmental changes
  • Coordination Excellence: Seamless cooperation with government security agencies
  • Operational Continuity: Business operations maintained without disruption

Successful Resolution

Outcome: Following seven days of continuous monitoring and heightened security, the identified threat subsided. No incidents were recorded during this period.

Current Status: Protective measures have been scaled back to standard operating levels, with continued routine monitoring in place.

Key Success Factors

  • Early Threat Detection: Proactive intelligence gathering identified risks before escalation
  • Rapid Response: Immediate deployment of protective measures within hours of threat identification
  • Zero Incidents: Complete protection achieved with no security breaches or safety concerns
  • Business Continuity: Client operations continued without interruption throughout the critical period
  • Multi-Agency Cooperation: Effective coordination with government and local security forces
